How it works

  • Explore Click anywhere on the map to get a 7-day weather forecast. Use the search bar to jump to any city.
  • Compare Add up to 6 cities to compare their weather side by side — temperature curves, humidity, wind.
  • Comfort 20 cities worldwide ranked by a comfort score combining temperature, humidity, wind & UV index.
  • Time Machine Pick any date since 1940 and a location to see what the weather was like that day.
